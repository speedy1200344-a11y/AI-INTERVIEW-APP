Project Name: AI Mock Interview Platform

What is this?
This is a Final Year Project that acts as a "Virtual Interviewer." Instead of being nervous in a real interview, students can practice with our AI to get better at answering questions.

How it works (Simple Steps):

Choose: Pick your job role (e.g., Frontend Developer).

Listen: The AI will ask you professional interview questions.

Answer: You can speak or type your answer.

Get Feedback: The AI will grade your answer and tell you how to improve.

Main Features:

✅ AI Interviewer: Gets you ready for real interviews.

✅ Voice System: You can speak to the AI like a real person.

✅ Smart Grading: Gives you a score and tips to get better.

✅ Easy to use: Simple, clean, and modern design.

Tech Used:

React.js (Frontend)

Google Gemini AI (The "Brain")

Firebase (To save your results)
